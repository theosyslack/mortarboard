Mortar Magic

Graduation has never been all about solemnity. There always has been the undercurrent of goose-bump joy — kept under raps until the recessional, when mortar boards fly. Today graduates take more pomp out of the circumstance by personalizing, painting and sequinning their mortar board billboards. “Hey, Mom.” “Thanks, Dad.” “#20 and #14.” Decipher the declarations of some members of the Class of 2014. Flip a mortar board to get acquainted.
